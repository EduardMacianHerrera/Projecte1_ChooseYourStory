Choose your story

Narrative adventure that will depend on random situations and on the user's decisions

What is choose your story?

Choose Your Story is a new game that combines narrative with a video game. You will live a story in which you will move through decisions in the face of situations that arise. Depending on the decisions you make you will have one end or the other.

The goal of the game is set for you personally depending on the situation before you, with complete freedom of choice. Do whatever you want, but be prepared to take on the consequences of your decisions.

How do you play?

The game follows a structure where a situation is shown in writing. When the situation arises, it asks you a question, usually to make a decision about what to do or confirm with a positive or negative answer.

The fact that the game does not show a graphical interface allows each player to imagine the game in their own way, making each game in a way unique and personal to each player.

Where to get it?

The game can be downloaded for free on our GitHub page. It is currently in its early stages, where it is free for all players to get to know it. We intend to expand with new stories and new choices within the stories, making it worthwhile to play again as a different story than the one you remembered.

all these extensions are available on our GitHub page and we will keep you informed through our social networks github.
